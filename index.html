Escape in Time

The clock is ticking

While cleaning out your grandmother's attic, you hear the faint soumd of a ticking clock. After rummaging around, you find the source - an ornate jewelry box. Upon opening the box, you discover a dusty pocket watch. You pick it up and are instantly transported to a time you do not recognize.

In order to return, you must solve the clues to discover what
month, date, and year
you have been transported to.
Good luck!
